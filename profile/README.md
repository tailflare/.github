  ### IGNITE YOUR FLARE

  **Tools for VTubers, virtual performers, and real-time creators.**
</div>

Tailflare is a new team building the infrastructure for the next generation of virtual creators.

From animation and motion processing to avatar technology and virtual production, we build systems that help people express themselves on the virtual stage.

Our goal is simple: to help creators bridge the gap between who they are and who they want to be, by giving them the tools to express themselves in real time without friction.

Much of our work is still in development, but we're beginning to share the building blocks of our technology. We believe the future of virtual creation is open and interoperable, free from fragmented tools, closed formats, and single-engine ecosystems. 

We're just getting started. Follow along as we build.
